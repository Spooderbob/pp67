Prerequisites

What you need before getting started

🖥 System Requirements

  • Windows 10 or 11 — 64-bit PC or laptop.
  • Android emulator — recommended emulator and download links provided in our Discord.
  • MLB The Show Companion App — installed via Google Play Store inside the emulator.

📱 App Setup

  • Currently supported item types: Diamond equipment, Sponsorships, and MLB Live Series cards.
  • The bot buys Diamond equipment from your favorites and MLB cards from your watchlist — tap the heart icon in the app to add items.
  • Items in your inventory will be automatically listed for sale.
Emulator Settings

Recommended configuration

Dial in these settings to give the bot the most stable surface to operate on.

🎨 Graphics & Display

  • Vulkan graphics renderer.
  • Tablet mode at 1920×1080, 280 DPI.
  • Optional: dynamic frame rate for background running.

📲 Device Model

  • Emulate a Samsung Galaxy S20 FE.

⚙ Other Settings

  • Remember window position.
  • Enable writable system disk.
  • Custom cursor enabled.
  • Disable PC hibernation while the emulator is running.

🔌 ADB Port Check

  • The emulator's ADB port must be 16384.
  • If different, update the value in the bot configuration to match.

Setup & Configuration

From first launch to fully tuned

🚀 First Launch

  • Run StubBot.exe.
  • Create a username and password — paste your license key to register. (Free version: no license key required.)
  • Credentials are stored securely for future logins.
  • Press F10 at any time to pause or resume the bot.

🎛 Configuration Panel

  • Toggle markets: Equipment, Sponsorships, MLB cards.
  • Set custom profit margins per item type.
  • Control inventory saturation with Max Buy Orders per Item.
  • Configure active hours, sleep limits, and random pauses for humanization.
  • Choose your console and set the emulator's ADB port.
Bot Loop Strategy

The five-step master loop

The standard cycle the bot runs through, end to end, before sleeping and starting over.

Step 01

Submit Buy Orders

Scrolls through favorited items and places buy orders.

Step 02

Revise Buy Orders

Reviews existing bids and updates them when they're no longer competitive.

Step 03

Check Inventory

Scans for newly acquired items and lists them for sale.

Step 04

Revise Sell Orders

Reviews your listings and undercuts the market where needed.

Step 05

Sleep & Repeat

Waits a randomized duration for humanization, then returns to Step 1.

Do's & Don'ts

Use the bot well

✓ Do

  • Manually update orders via the companion app, website or in-game.
  • Place your own orders manually — the bot is designed to work alongside them.
  • Keep your favorites and watchlist tidy and intentional.

✕ Don't

  • Have large quantities of unsupported item orders (e.g., Unlockables, Stadiums) — it can confuse the bot.
  • Run the bot on environments where automation isn't authorized.
  • Share your local credentials or session data with anyone.
