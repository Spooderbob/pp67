How It Works

ShowFlip handles the analysis. You handle the trades.

1

Market Data is Scanned

ShowFlip scans the MLB The Show 26 community marketplace every 5 minutes, tracking buy-now prices, order books, and sell velocity for cards across all rarities.

2

Flip Opportunities Are Identified

The algorithm detects price gaps — cards selling below their true market value. It calculates exact profit after the 10% tax and assigns a confidence score based on trend and volume.

3

Picks Are Published Live

Only high-confidence opportunities appear on the feed. Each pick shows the buy price, sell target, expected profit, and the reasoning behind the call.

4

You Execute the Trade

Open the in-game marketplace, buy the card at or below the listed buy price, then list it at the sell price. Collect your stubs when it sells.
